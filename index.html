WEminder captures what you say you need, organizes it, and reminds you at the right place and time — so you don’t miss milk, dog treats, batteries, anything.

How It Works

WEminder listens to everyday life — “We’re out of paper towels,” “We need AAA batteries,” “We should grab more dog treats” — and turns that into organized, shareable lists. Then it reminds you when you’re near a place that actually sells the thing you need.

Speak Naturally

Just say it in normal conversation — “We’re out of milk.” WEminder captures it and adds it for you.

Smart Location Alerts

When you’re near a store that has what you need, you get a nudge. Right place, right moment.

Shared Lists

Your family / partner / roommate stays synced. Everyone sees what’s needed in real time.

For Shoppers, Families & Brands

WEminder connects real shopper intent (“I need dog treats”) with real offers, in real time. That means better trips for families and smarter targeting for brands.
